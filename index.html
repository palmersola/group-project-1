WHO ARE WE

Welcome to Stink Inc! A family-owned business that has been selling stinky smells for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality smells at affordable prices. Everyone should have access to great-smelling stinks, and we work hard to ensure our customers are satisfied with every purchase.

We offer many stinky smells, including farts, ammonia, and gangrene. Our products are made with only the finest ingredients, and we take great care to ensure that they are safe for use by everyone.

At Stink Inc, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service. If you have any questions or concerns about our products or services, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We are always happy to help!
